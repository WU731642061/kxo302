Problems

Impact of the takeout industry on the environment

Nowadays, the takeout market is becoming more and more popular among customers, especially students, which also including students in Lingang. Behind the huge orders, the waste of resources and environmental pollution should not be ignored. Takeout businesses are fighting fiercely for the takeout market, which brings about the surge of disposable chopsticks, lunch boxes and plastic bags, resulting in a lot of waste of resources and white pollution. And many researches point out that the current takeout garbage basic unmanned recovery status. While us students in Lingang enjoying the convenience of living in the O2O industry, such as the service and convenience provided by the takeout industry, they have to pay much for the environmental costs. How can we find a balance between increasing the convenience of living and reducing the environmental pollution? It is urgent for the community to pay attention and think.

How to reduce the waste?
a) Short-term: Try to guide users and merchants to consciously reduce the use of disposable tableware.
b) Medium-term: Comprehensive utilization of economic and technological means to improve the enthusiasm of businesses to use environmentally-friendly tableware
c) Long-term: In-depth supply chain, with demand-side advances forcing the supply chain reform.
